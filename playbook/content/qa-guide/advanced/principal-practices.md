# Principal QA: Organization-Wide Quality Vision

## Strategic Framework 1: Quality Maturity Model
Define 5-stage quality maturity model from initial (ad-hoc testing) to optimizing (predictive quality analytics).

## Strategic Framework 2: AI-Powered Quality Intelligence
Build ML models to predict defect-prone areas, optimize test selection, and automate quality insights.

## Strategic Framework 3: Quality-as-Code Culture
Transform quality from gatekeeper role to embedded practice with automated gates and continuous feedback.
