# Senior Product Owner: Advanced Backlog & Stakeholder Management

## Pattern 1: AI-Assisted User Story Generation
Use ChatGPT to generate well-structured user stories with acceptance criteria, then refine with domain knowledge.

## Pattern 2: Backlog Prioritization with AI Insights
Leverage AI to analyze customer feedback, market trends, and business metrics for data-driven prioritization.

## Pattern 3: Sprint Planning Optimization
Use AI to estimate story points, identify dependencies, and optimize sprint capacity planning.
