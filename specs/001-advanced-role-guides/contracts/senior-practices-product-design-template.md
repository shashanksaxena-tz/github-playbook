## Mentorship & Domain Excellence

As a senior professional, you're expected to mentor others and elevate quality across your team.

### AI-Assisted Quality Review

**Domain-Specific Review**:
- Use AI to validate completeness of deliverables
- Example prompts:
  - Product Owner: "Review this user story for completeness. Check acceptance criteria, edge cases, and testability."
  - Product Manager: "Analyze this feature proposal for market fit, competitive positioning, and risk factors."
  - UI Designer: "Review this design for accessibility compliance, responsive behavior, and design system consistency."
  - UX Designer: "Evaluate this research plan for completeness, bias prevention, and actionable insights potential."

**Cross-Functional Communication**:
- Use AI to translate technical concepts for different audiences
- Generate documentation that serves multiple stakeholder groups
- Create presentations tailored to different experience levels

### Mentoring Junior Professionals

**Teaching AI Tool Usage**:
1. **Show, Don't Tell**: Demonstrate your prompting process in collaboration sessions
2. **Explain Your Review**: When reviewing AI-generated work, explain what you're validating and why
3. **Set Boundaries**: Teach when NOT to use AI (sensitive stakeholder conversations, highly confidential work, nuanced strategic decisions)

**Common Questions to Address**:
- Q: "Should I use this AI-generated deliverable as-is?"
  - A: "Review it like any work product. Validate against domain knowledge, user needs, and business constraints."
- Q: "The AI generated something that seems good but I'm not confident. Should I use it?"
  - A: "No. You must be able to defend your work. Refine it until you fully understand and can advocate for every decision."

**Pair Working with AI**:
- Collaborate with junior team members on AI-assisted tasks
- Walk through your prompting iteration process
- Show how you validate, refine, and integrate AI suggestions
- Explain your domain expertise signals that catch AI errors

## Measuring Success

Track these metrics to evaluate your effectiveness as a senior {role} using AI tools:

### Individual Contribution
- **Deliverable Quality**: Stakeholder satisfaction with your outputs (target: 4.5/5 average)
- **Decision Impact**: Accuracy of your strategic recommendations (track validation rate)
- **Efficiency**: Time to create high-quality deliverables (track trend, not absolute speed)

### Mentorship Impact
- **Knowledge Transfer**: Number of team members comfortable using advanced AI patterns (target: 50%+ within 6 months)
- **Quality Improvement**: Improvement in deliverable quality from professionals you mentor
- **AI Adoption**: Team members using AI effectively without compromising domain expertise

### Professional Influence
- **Pattern Adoption**: Number of advanced patterns you've introduced that become team standards
- **Documentation**: Quality and usage of frameworks/templates you create
- **Problem Solving**: Time to resolution for complex domain challenges (track trend)

### Role-Specific Metrics

**Product Owner**:
- Sprint goal achievement rate
- User story acceptance rate on first review
- Backlog health (clear, estimated, prioritized)

**Product Manager**:
- Feature success rate (adoption, usage, satisfaction)
- Time from concept to market
- Cross-functional stakeholder satisfaction

**UI Designer**:
- Design system adoption rate
- Time to prototype high-fidelity designs
- Accessibility compliance score

**UX Designer**:
- Research insights leading to product changes
- User satisfaction improvements
- Time from research to actionable recommendations

### Self-Assessment Questions
- Are you explaining your decisions clearly to junior team members?
- Are your AI-assisted deliverables more thorough than manually-created ones?
- Do you consistently validate AI outputs against domain expertise?
- Are you balancing speed (using AI) with quality (thorough review and refinement)?
